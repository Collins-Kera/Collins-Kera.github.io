Welcome to Rexburg Orthodontics

Dr. Summers and his friendly staff welcome the opportunity to help you find a healthy, beautiful smile. Dr. Summers is an orthodontic speacialist committed to providing excellent care in a comfortable, relaxed environment.

Dr. Summers offers a wide variety of treatment from early childhood to adult smile corretion. Whether you choose traditional metal braces, clear braces, or the Invisalign system, Dr. Summers’ expertise will bring out the smile in you.
